Dice Vaults: giant hollow D20s that hold tons of your regular dice.

What these are: The Dice Vault is a large, 3D printed D20 that's hollow inside with a fitted lid. Pop the top off and stash tons of your regular dice inside. It works great as a display piece, a gift, or just a fun way to keep your whole collection in one place.

Every Dice Vault is designed from scratch, 3D printed, then assembled by hand, one at a time.

Designed From Scratch

Every Dice Vault starts as a full CAD model. Each triangular panel is designed, numbered, and laid out before anything gets printed.
